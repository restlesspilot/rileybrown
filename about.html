About Riley Brown

Riley Brown has established himself as "The Number One AI Educator," dedicating his career to making artificial intelligence accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background. With a significant following across multiple social media platforms, Riley has become a trusted voice in the AI education space.

Background

Riley recently moved to San Francisco to launch his startup VibeCode, working alongside co-founders Ansh Nanda and Kehan Zhang. His journey into AI education began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between complex AI technologies and practical, everyday applications.

Philosophy

Riley believes that "one of the reasons why people don't get deep into computer science is they don't get hit with the 'aha' moment soon enough." His approach focuses on creating those breakthrough moments through practical, hands-on learning experiences.

Current Focus

Today, Riley is at the forefront of several revolutionary movements in AI:

  • Pioneering "Vibe Coding" - a method of building applications using only AI assistance
  • Developing accessible AI education content for non-technical audiences
  • Building VibeCode, a platform that democratizes app development
  • Exploring the future of AI-powered video editing and content creation

Through his work, Riley continues to inspire creators and entrepreneurs to leverage AI tools, making the seemingly impossible accessible to everyone.
